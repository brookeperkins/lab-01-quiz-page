NOW THIS IS A STORY ALL ABOUT MEOW...

NOMI

Nomi is my first cat. When I lived in New York, his mother had her litter of kittens beneath my stoop, quickly abandoning them. I rescued the four kittens, and nursed them to health. My friends adopted the other kittens when they were old enough, and I kept Nomi. He is now five years old. He is orange, with white swirls along his belly and back. He loves to cuddle, and despite my best efforts, he remains a pretty chunky guy.

LEAF

My partner adopted Leaf from the humane society a few months after we had moved to Portland. He is also orange, but with darker orange swirls. He was just a kitten when we adopted him, and although he is now a year old, he remains relatively small. He is incredibly vocal about his struggles, his needs, and his innermost desires, although it is hard to distinguish between the three.
